About JJ

JJ's Custom PCs is built around a long-running passion for building, fixing, and optimizing PCs. Projects range from budget-friendly gaming rigs to powerful workstations, with clear guidance throughout the process.

The mission is to help customers get the right PC without the stress. Whether the project is a budget build, a high-end gaming machine, or an assembly using parts already selected, the process stays focused on performance, fit, and reliability.

Every build emphasizes precision, performance, and aesthetics, so the finished PC runs smoothly and looks clean at the same time.
